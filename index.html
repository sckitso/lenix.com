Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 30, 2026

At Lenix, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Lenix does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information such as your name, email, or phone number. However, certain data may be collected automatically through third-party services integrated into the app, as described below.

Advertising

Lenix displays ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as the Advertising Identifier / IDFA)
  • Approximate (coarse) location
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
  • App usage and performance data

If you grant permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt, personalized ads may be shown based on your activity across other apps and websites. You can change this at any time in your device Settings. If you do not grant permission, you will still see ads, but they will not be personalized.

Analytics

Lenix uses Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used in aggregate. This may include data such as app opens, session duration, and general usage patterns. This data is collected anonymously and is not linked to your personal identity.

Subscriptions

Lenix offers optional subscriptions to remove ads. Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data collected by third-party services (Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics) is governed by their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy

Security

Your privacy and security are our priority. We do not store personal information on our servers. Any data collected by third-party services is handled according to their security practices.

Children's Privacy

Lenix is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
